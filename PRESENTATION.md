## Presentation Notes:

### Delivery Tips:
- **Timing**: 15-20 minutes total
- **Focus**: Emphasize the "zero backend" innovation
- **Demo**: Live demo is crucial for impact
- **Stories**: Use real-world examples of current pain points

### Key Messages to Emphasize:
1. **90% cost reduction** vs traditional solutions
2. **60-second deployment** vs months for competitors
3. **Complete privacy** vs data concerns with cloud solutions
4. **Enterprise features** in a client-side package

### Anticipated Questions:
- **Scalability**: How does it handle thousands of users?
- **Security**: How is data protected client-side?
- **Integration**: How does it connect with existing tools?
- **Migration**: How do teams switch from current solutions?
